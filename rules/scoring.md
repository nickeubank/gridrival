## 🏎️ Performance Bonuses

### Overtake Points

* **+3 Points** per position gained.
* *Note:* Awarded for net positions gained between **Qualifying Position** (not grid start position) and **Race Finish Position**.

### Improvement Points

Awarded when a driver finishes above their **8-race average finish position**.

| Positions Improved | Points |
| :--- | :--- |
| **1** | 0 |
| **2** | 2 |
| **3** | 4 |
| **4** | 6 |
| **5** | 9 |
| **6** | 12 |
| **7** | 16 |
| **8** | 20 |
| **9** | 25 |
| **10 - 22** | 30 |

### Beating Teammate Points

Awarded to the driver who finishes ahead of their teammate(s).

| Win Margin (Positions) | Points |
| :--- | :--- |
| **1-3 positions** | 2 |
| **4-7 positions** | 5 |
| **8-12 positions** | 8 |
| **13+ positions** | 12 |

### Completion Bonus

Points are awarded at specific race milestones.

| Completion % | Points |
| :--- | :--- |
| **25%** | 3 points |
| **50%** | 3 points |
| **75%** | 3 points |
| **90%** | 3 points |

> **Calculation Rule:** GridRival divides the total laps by these percentages and rounds down. For example, in a 78-lap race, the 75% bonus is awarded after the 58th lap.

## 🏗️ Constructor Scoring

Constructors earn points based on each of their two drivers' qualifying and race results. Points are awarded per driver and summed. Constructors do **not** earn overtake, improvement, teammate, or completion bonuses.
