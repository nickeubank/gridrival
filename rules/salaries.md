# GridRival Salary Guide

## Salary Adjustment Details

Salary adjustments are determined through a three-step process after each event:

• Step 1: Elements are ranked according to the fantasy points they've earned during the event.

• Step 2: This ranking is compared to the "default salary table" to determine the "base salary variation". This is the difference between an element's salary before the race and the salary associated with their fantasy rank after the race.

• Step 3: The base salary variation is divided by 4 and rounded down to the nearest 100k to determine the final adjustment amount.

  • Maximum Increase/Decrease: +/- £2M for drivers and +/- £3M for constructors.

  • Minimum Adjustment: +/- £100k for both [cite: 8].

## More details presentation

For the data-obsessed scientists, engineers, and mathematicians out there, here is a detailed breakdown of exactly how driver salaries are determined. 😀

Step 1:

After each event, we rank elements according to the fantasy points they've earned during the event.

Step 2:

We then compare this ranking to our “default salary table” (shown below) to determine the "base salary variation." Salary variation is the difference between a driver or constructor's salary before the race, to the salary associated with their fantasy rank after the race using the salary shown on the "default salary table" below. For example, if a driver's salary prior to a race was $8.0M and their fantasy rank for that race was 14th ($10.9M on the default salary table), the base salary variation would be +$2.9M.

Step 3:

Finally, we divide the base salary variation by 4 and round down to the nearest 100k to determine the final adjustment amount. The maximum increase is +/- $2M for drivers, +/- $3M for constructors and the minimum adjustment is +/- $100k for both.
​

Salary Adjustment Example
Driver's salary before the race: $15.8M

Driver's fantasy rank for the race: 8th

Default salary for 8th place (based on default salary table): $22.8M

Salary variation between steps #1 & #3: +$7M

Adjustment amount: (+$7M / 4) = +$1.75

New Salary after Round 5 (#1 + #5): +$17.55

All elements will start the season with a manually calculated starting salary based on the previous season's performance, pre-season testing, and/or projected performance.
